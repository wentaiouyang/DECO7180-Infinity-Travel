Mission briefing

Survive the time tunnel

Hey there, pilot! You are strapped inside a time machine that just booted up. The tunnel is unstable, riddled with floating obstacles and hidden relics. React fast, keep your hovercraft grounded, and the tunnel might just let you through.

  • Arcade-tight controls: Tap SPACE at the perfect moment to dodge the debris.
  • Procedural obstacles: Every run spawns a fresh path, so there is always a new pattern to learn.
  • Unlockable memories: Snap rare photos during your run and build a gallery of timelines you saved.
